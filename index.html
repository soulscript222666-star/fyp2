About Tradify

Tradify is a Final Year Project developed by BSIT students (Session 2022–2026) at the Institute of Computer Science and Information Technology, University of Agriculture, Peshawar.

The platform addresses a real gap in Pakistan's digital finance ecosystem — the lack of a unified, risk-free, locally-built trading education and community platform combining live charts, portfolio simulation, and knowledge sharing.

Built using the MERN stack with Firebase backend, TradingView chart integration, and real-time Firestore sync.

  • Live market data for Gold, Bitcoin, Forex, and Global Stocks
  • Paper trading simulation with virtual PKR 100,000 balance
  • Community-driven trading analysis and blogging platform
  • TradingView-powered professional charts with multiple timeframes
  • Price alert system with in-app notifications
  • Firebase Auth + Firestore real-time backend
  • Fullscreen & resizable live chart on Charts page
  • Designed for Pakistani context with local market focus
